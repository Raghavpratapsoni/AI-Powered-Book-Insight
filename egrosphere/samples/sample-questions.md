# Sample Questions

Use these prompts after importing scraped books or uploading `samples/books/library-mystery.txt`.

## Book-specific prompts

- What is the central conflict in this book?
- Summarize the book in three sentences.
- Which genre best fits this story and why?
- What tone does the description suggest?

## Cross-book prompts

- Which imported book feels the most reflective or melancholic?
- If I liked the selected book, what should I read next?
- What themes appear across multiple books in the dataset?
- Which book has the strongest mystery elements?
